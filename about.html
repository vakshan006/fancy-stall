Our Story ✨

A Small Stall with Big Dreams

Style U Kadai is more than just a stall - it's a student-run initiative born from a love for fashion and a desire for affordability. We started this small business at Oxford College to bring trendy accessories to our fellow students without breaking the bank.

Our Mission 🎯

To provide high-quality, trendy, and budget-friendly fashion accessories to everyone who wants to express their unique style.

Why Us? 💖

We hand-select every item, ensuring they are not only stylish but also priced fairly for students and young fashion lovers.

Whether you're looking for that perfect pair of Jimikis for a function or a cute scrunchie for an everyday look, we've got you covered. Come visit us at our stall and play the Lucky Spin for extra surprises!
